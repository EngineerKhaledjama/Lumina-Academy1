Who We Are

Built by Learners,
for Learners

Lumina Academy was founded in 2020 by a team of engineers, designers, and educators who believed the best education shouldn't be locked behind expensive university tuition or geography.

We partner with industry leaders at Google, Figma, McKinsey, and AWS to create courses that are practical, current, and genuinely career-changing — not just theory you'll forget.

Today, over 12,000 learners in 80+ countries have transformed their careers through Lumina. From absolute beginners to senior professionals, our community learns together, grows together, and succeeds together.
